About Me

Where do I begin? I'm a Mexican-American woman raised in California who is obsessed with food and good TV, subjectively speaking, of course. I am a writer, at heart; I am proud to say my most important piece on underreported sexual assault cases on college campuses was published. When I'm not reading, writing, or watching my beloved shows, you will find me working at one of my three jobs in childcare. Believe it or not, a big part of my Mexican grandmothers remain concerned with the fact that I'm 25 years old and continue to remain unmarried; my coping mechanism for the 'concerned' opinions I get to hear from my large family during the Holidays, is that I get to live this independent life for young women my grandmother's once were. When they were each 16 and 17 they married men because that was their only way to savor a slice of freedom. That freedom came with 10 children, each, and a lifetime of disappointments. So here is to Ofelia and Clara.
